Opera Puzzle – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-03

Opera Puzzle is a puzzle game inspired by Peking Opera face art. This policy explains what data the app handles and how it is used.

Summary

  • No account or sign-in required.
  • No ads, analytics SDKs, or third-party trackers.
  • Your game progress and settings stay on your device.
  • No in-app purchases.
  • Network access is used only when you open external links or email support.

Data Stored on Your Device

To provide core features, we store the following locally:

  • Game progress (chapters/levels completed).
  • Unlocked characters, gallery, and achievements.
  • Preferences such as sound and language settings.
  • Local save data needed to run the game.

You can remove all data by deleting the app.

Photos & Files

Opera Puzzle does not access your photo library or files.

What We Do Not Collect

  • No personal identifiers (name, email, phone, precise location).
  • No contacts, camera, microphone, or calendar access.
  • No advertising identifiers or behavioral tracking.

External Links

The app may include a link to support email. Opening this link is optional and handled by your device or mail app.

Children’s Privacy

Opera Puzzle is intended for a general audience and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Security

Your data stays on your device within the iOS sandbox. Please keep your device updated and protected.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date above and publishing the new version at this URL.

Contact
